Faceted search

The faceted search allows you to browse and sort ESGF datasets in any custom hierarchical order. Explore and drill down search results until you have found what your looking for.

How to filter

  1. Select one of the filters
  2. A list with possible settings appear, enable one ore more settings by clicking once. Clicking again disables the setting.
  3. Click the "Search" button, the selection is adjusted.
  4. Click on a dataset, its catalog is shown in a dialog
Filter tips

It is good practice to select a project first, for example CORDEX. Second step could be selecting a variable (tas) and time frequency (mon). This reduces the number of search results already.
E.g: project Cordex --> time_frequency mon --> variable tas reduces the number of results from 420824 to 387.
